Key Lime Pie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup key lime juice or 1 lb key limes
  • 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 9" grahm cracker pie crust

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350.

Crack eggs and separate yolk from whites. Add yolks to bowl.

If using fresh limes, juice them over a measuring cup to a half cup.

Add sweetened condensed milk, egg yolks, and key lime juice to a medium mixing bowl. Beat thoroughly with a whisk or fork to mix until smooth.

Pour mixture into pie crust and bake for 15 minutes at 350ยบ.

Allow pie to stand for 10 minutes before refrigerating, covered, for more than two hours. Serve with whipped cream and lime slices.
